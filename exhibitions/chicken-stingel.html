Chicken Stingel

Exhibition Date: Sunday May 25th 2025 - 21st June 2025

The one-day-only live painting performance featured a series of canvases and digitally developed copper casts. Installed primarily in a chicken coop, the works by Kenny Schachter, Maura and Margaret—a pair of Gallus gallus domesticus—are available to view by appointment through June 21, 2025.

The exhibition draws on Rudolf Stingel's 2002 and 2012 series of Celotex insulation panel works and their later electroformed copper casts. Here, chickens were invited to collaborate on micro-canvases using edible glue and seed. The resulting works were scanned and will later be cast in silver and gold.
