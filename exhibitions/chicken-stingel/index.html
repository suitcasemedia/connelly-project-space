Chicken Stingel

The one-day-only live painting performance features a series of canvases and digitally developed copper casts. Installed primarily in a chicken coop, the works by Kenny Schachter, Maura and Margaret—a pair of Gallus gallus domesticus—are available to view by appointment through June 21, 2025.

The exhibition draws on Rudolf Stingel's Celotex insulation panel works. Here, chickens collaborated on micro-canvases using edible glue and seed. The resulting works were scanned and will later be cast in silver and gold.
