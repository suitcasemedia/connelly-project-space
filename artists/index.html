Artists

  • Zara Connelly – Founder. Emerging artist exploring portraiture, identity, and landscape, particularly through the lens of chicken life in 2025.
  • Kenny Schachter – Celebrated artist and curator, known for pushing boundaries and collaborating with non-human participants.

  • Elena Connelly – Artist and Co-Founder of Connelly Project Space, with a focus on family-led, interspecies creative exploration. Also brilliant at making dollhouses out of cardboard.
  • Jimmy Connelly – Co-Founder and curator of Connelly Project Space, artist and organiser, supporting new forms of collaborative art practice.
