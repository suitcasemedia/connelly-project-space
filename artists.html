Artists

  • Zara Connelly – Emerging artist exploring portraiture, identity, and landscape, particularly through the lens of chicken life in 2025.
  • Kenny Schachter – Celebrated artist and curator, known for pushing boundaries and collaborating with non-human participants.
  • Maura & Margaret – A pair of chickens and key contributors to the Chicken Stingel exhibition, blending instinctive mark-making with conceptual art.
  • Elena Connelly – Artist and co-organiser of Connelly Project Space, with a focus on family-led, interspecies creative exploration also brilliant at making dolls houses out of carboard.
  • Jimmy Connelly – Founder and curator of Connelly Project Space, artist and organiser, supporting new forms of collaborative art practice.
